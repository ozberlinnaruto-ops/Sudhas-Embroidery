What We Teach

Ancient Crafts,
Timeless Beauty

Aari and Zardoshi are two of India's most celebrated textile embroidery traditions — intricate, painstaking, and breathtakingly beautiful. Our academy has been teaching these arts for over 8 years, producing skilled artisans, boutique designers, and home entrepreneurs.

Whether you want to learn for personal fulfilment, start a business, or reach professional-grade skill — we have the right course for you.

🪡

Aari Embroidery

Chain-stitch needle work creating intricate motifs on stretched fabric — used in sarees, blouses, dupattas, and home décor.

✨

Zardoshi Work

Gold & silver thread embellishment — opulent, dazzling, and synonymous with Indian bridal couture and luxury fashion.

📿

Embroidery Jewellery

A modern fusion — Aari techniques applied to create unique wearable jewellery with excellent commercial potential.
