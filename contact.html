Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

No prior experience is needed for our Basic Aari or Basic Zardoshi courses. They are designed specifically for complete beginners — we start from zero, covering material selection, frame setup, and your very first stitches.

Recorded courses let you learn at your own pace — watch and re-watch lessons anytime on ClassPlus. Live courses have scheduled sessions with the instructor on Zoom/Google Meet, allowing real-time questions, feedback, and peer interaction. Both are available on ClassPlus.

After your payment is confirmed, you will receive your ClassPlus course access link via email and WhatsApp within 30 minutes. Simply download the ClassPlus app, log in, and your course will be ready. Recorded courses start immediately; Live courses will include the batch schedule and joining details.

A detailed materials list is provided for each course after enrolment. For Aari, you'll need an Aari needle, frame, and thread. For Zardoshi, you'll need a tambour frame and specified metallic threads. We also recommend affordable beginner kits available on Indian e-commerce platforms.

Yes, no-cost EMI options of 3, 6, and 12 months are available for courses above ₹3,000. This is processed through the payment gateway at the time of checkout. Simply select EMI as your payment method in the enrolment form and we'll share the eligible EMI payment link.

We offer a 7-day refund policy for recorded courses if you haven't accessed more than 20% of the content. For Live courses, refunds are available if requested at least 48 hours before the first session. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email to process a refund request.
